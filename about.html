About Us

Strand's Ski Shop in Worcester, MA has been serving the skiing and snowboarding community for over 30 years. We pride ourselves on offering a huge selection of ski and snowboard equipment, apparel, helmets, boots, bindings, and more.

Our experienced and trained staff are passionate about winter sports and are here to help you find the perfect gear for your needs. We offer a variety of services, including:

  • Expert custom boot fitting
  • Ski and snowboard tune-ups
  • Binding mounting and repairs
  • Waxing and edge sharpening

Whether you're a seasoned pro or a beginner, we're here to make sure you have the best experience on the slopes. Visit us today and let us help you gear up for your next adventure!
